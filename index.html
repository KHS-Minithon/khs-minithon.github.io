About

Making a difference

Who Are We?

We are a group of KHS students dedicated to raise as much money as possible to fight childhood cancer.

What Do We Do?

The KHS Mini-THON raises money through fundraisers and our annual Mini-THON to benefit the Four Diamonds Research.

Why We Do It?

We do it for the fight. For the cure. For The Kids living with cancer.

Since When?

We have been fighting childhood cancer since 2015 with our first annual Mini-THON being held in February of 2016.

What we do

Mini-THONs

Mini-THONs are multi-hour, interactive, fun-filled events that are completely student-led. They can also be 3 month projects or year-long activities where students raise awareness and funds for Four Diamonds.

Donations

By raising money, we can assure that the children get the help they need and deserve. Thanks to donations, 100% of children with cancer at Penn State Hershey Children's Hospital are eligible for assistance.

Raising Awareness

By reaching out the community, we make everyone come together to help fight for this noble cause.

Research

Through Four Diamonds, Penn State Hershey Children's Hospital is seeking improved treatments and cures to benefit those around the world.
